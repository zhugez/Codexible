# DESIGN DNA — Codexible

## Brand Personality
- Technical, confident, minimal
- High signal, low fluff
- Feels fast and precise

## Visual Direction
- Dark-first neutral foundation
- Strong but restrained accent color
- Soft radius, clean borders, subtle depth

## Typography
- Heading: bold, compact line-height
- Body: medium contrast for long readability
- Code/install snippets: monospaced with high contrast

## Spacing & Rhythm
- 8px scale
- Generous vertical spacing between sections
- Tight spacing inside dense technical cards

## Component Language
- Cards: bordered, subtle hover lift
- Buttons:
  - Primary: solid accent
  - Secondary: ghost/outline
- Modals: layered backdrop, clear close and primary action

## Motion
- Fast, small-amplitude transitions (150–250ms)
- No distracting parallax/heavy animation

## Accessibility Baseline
- Ensure CTA/button contrast passes AA
- Focus-visible styles on interactive controls
- Modal keyboard close and focus trap behavior

## Content Tone
- Direct and concrete
- Avoid generic marketing adjectives
- Every section should answer: "Why should a dev care?"
